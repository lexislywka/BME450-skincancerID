# BME450-skincancerID
Lexi Slywka (lexislywka)

For my project, I am planning on making a neural network that can analyze different skin lesions to determine if they are cancerous. The hope is that the network can recognize multiple different cancerous lesions including basal cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, and melanoma. Detecting these cancerous lesions on non-white skin tones is oftentimes underresearched/ underrepresented so the hope would be to train the neural network on a wide range of skin tones and cancer types. There are some datasets on Kaggle of different cancerous skin lesions that I plan to use. I may have to supplement some additional photos to include a wide range of skin tones
